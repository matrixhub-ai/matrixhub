# Create and Delete Models/Datasets

## Prerequisites

- A valid MatrixHub account.
- Read/Write permissions in the target project.

## Creating Models

1. Go to **Project Management** and select your project.
1. Click the **Model Repository** tab.
1. Click **Create Model**, enter the name, and confirm.

## Creating Datasets

1. Go to **Project Management** and select your project.
1. Click the **Dataset** tab.
1. Click **Create Dataset**, enter the name, and confirm.

## Deleting Assets

1. Navigate to the **Model Settings** or **Dataset Settings** tab.
1. Click **Delete** and follow the confirmation prompts.

:::warning

Deletion is permanent. Ensure you have backups before deleting any assets.

:::
